About Amy

Amy Grant holds a B.A. in Art History from Marymount Manhattan College. She started her career in NYC, working in picture research and then serving in one of the curatorial departments of The Metropolitan Museum of Art. After marrying and starting to raise children in NJ, Amy and her family relocated to suburban Philadelphia.

Settling in a community near Valley Forge National Park, she discovered many new and exciting opportunities to continue studying Art. She shifted her focus from Art History to Studio Art, taking classes and workshops at the Wayne Art Center and the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts. This course work lead to a new career in Art Education.

Amy has had the privilege of teaching Art to children for over twenty years. Amy now devotes her time to practicing her own artwork, focusing primarily on florals, still-lifes and landscapes in pastel.
