About the Artist

Amy Grant holds a B.A. in Art History from Marymount Manhattan College. She started her career in NYC as a picture researcher for college Art textbooks. After marrying and starting to raise her children in NJ, Amy and her family relocated to suburban Philadelphia.

Settling in Chester County, PA, she discovered new opportunities to continue studying Art. She shifted her focus from Art History to Studio Art, taking classes and workshops at the Wayne Art Center and the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts. These classes opened doors to a new career-- Art Education.

Amy has had the privilege of teaching Art to children for over twenty years. Amy now devotes her time to practicing her own artwork, focusing primarily on florals, still-lifes, and landscapes in pastel.
